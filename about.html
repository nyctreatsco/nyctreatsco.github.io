About

Hi! I'm Emily. I've loved all things sweet for as long as I can remember, and ventured into the Instagram foodie influencer world in 2014 as NYC Treats. In a city as big as New York is, there's an event greater amount of creativity, passion, and love for food. My mission is to find and share with my followers the very best of the best, from big names to hidden gems, and everything in between.

I began baking from a young age and never stopped, always eager to experiment in the kitchen and come up with new recipes. Cookies were a childhood favorite of mine, and each cookie I create to this day is hand-crafted to perfection. Join me on this cookie journey, and I'll promise you've never had anything like a NYC Treats cookie before!

Frosted Animal Cookie

Sugar cookie stuffed with circus animal cookies, white chocolate chips, and rainbow sprinkles. Topped with a dollop of vanilla funfetti frosting and a circus animal cookie.

S'mores Cookie

Milk chocolate chip cookie filled with graham crackers, mini marshmallows, and milk chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and Hershey's milk chocolate.

White Chocolate Reese's Pieces Cookie

White chocolate cookie filled with Reese's Pieces, white chocolate chips, and peanut butter chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and a Reese's Peanut Butter Cup.

Dunkaroo Cookie

Sugar cookie filled with Dunkaroo vanilla cookies and funfetti white chocolate chips. Stuffed with funfetti frosting. Topped with a dollop of Dunkaroo funfetti frosting and a Dunkaroo cookie.

Cookies n' Cream Cookie

White chocolate cookie stuffed with Hershey's Cookies 'n' Creme chocolate and white chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and Hershey's Cookies 'n' Creme chocolate.

OG Chocolate Chip Cookie

Chocolate chip cookie filled with semi-sweet chocolate chips, chocolate chunks, and milk chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of creamy & rich chocolate frosting and Hershey's milk chocolate.

Frosted Animal Cookie

Sugar cookie stuffed with circus animal cookies, white chocolate chips, and rainbow sprinkles. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and a circus animal cookie.

S'mores Cookie

Milk chocolate chip cookie stuffed with graham crackers, mini marshmallows, and milk chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and Hershey's milk chocolate.

White Chocolate Reese's Pieces Cookie

White chocolate cookie stuffed with Reese's Pieces, white chocolate chips, and peanut butter chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and Reese's Pieces.

Dunkaroo Cookie

Sugar cookie stuffed with Dunkaroo vanilla cookies, funfetti white chocolate chips, and rainbow circus sprinkles. Filled with funfetti frosting. Topped with a dollop of Dunkaroo funfetti frosting and a vanilla cookie.

Cookies n' Cream Cookie

White chocolate cookie stuffed with Hershey's Cookies 'n' Creme chocolate, Oreo cookies, and white chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of whipped vanilla frosting and Hershey's Cookies 'n' Creme chocolate.

OG Chocolate Chip Cookie

Chocolate chip cookie stuffed with semi-sweet chocolate chips, chocolate chunks, and milk chocolate chips. Topped with a dollop of creamy chocolate frosting and Hershey's milk chocolate.

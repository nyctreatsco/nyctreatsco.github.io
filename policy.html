What is special about an NYC Treats cookie?

Our cookies are carefully handcrafted and made fresh from scratch daily. Quality means everything to us, so our cookies are all natural and made with the finest certified organic ingredients.

How will my cookies be shipped?

Each cookie is individually wrapped and placed in a box to ensure that cookies stay safe during transportation.

Where do you ship to?

We ship nationwide!

Which shipping service is used, and when should I expect my package?

All orders are shipped through USPS Priority Mail or USPS First-Class Mail. Most packages arrive within 1-3 business days depending on the carrier. Order confirmation and tracking information will be provided by email.

What is your allergy information?

All cookies are made with equipment that processes wheat, soy, milk, peanut, and tree nuts.

Could I cancel my order after it has been placed?

If you wish to cancel an order, you must contact NYC Treats by email within an hour of placing your order. All sales are final after that.

What is your return policy?

Each cookie is handcrafted with time and dedication. Returns are not accepted and all sales are final.

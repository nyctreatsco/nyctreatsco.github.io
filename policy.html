Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping

If you wish to cancel an order you placed, you must contact NYC Treats either by email or DM within an hour of placing your order. All sales are final after that.

Cookies are custom made Friday-Sunday, and are shipped the same day or the following day.

All cookies are made with equipment that processes wheat, soy, milk, peanut, and tree nuts.

All orders are shipped through USPS Priority Mail. Tracking information will be provided by email.

Each cookie is individually wrapped and placed in a box to ensure that cookies stay in place during transportation.

Returns

Each cookie is handcrafted with time and dedication. Returns are not accepted and all sales are final.
